Welcome To Doha!

Doha, the capital of Qatar, is a fascinating blend of modernity and tradition. With its gleaming skyscrapers, luxurious malls, and bustling souqs, Doha offers visitors a unique cultural experience.

Explore the stunning Museum of Islamic Art, stroll along the Corniche promenade, and marvel at the futuristic architecture of the Doha skyline. Discover the vibrant markets of Souq Waqif and indulge in delicious Qatari cuisine at local eateries.

Best Time To Visit

November to April.

Mild weather, perfect for outdoor activities.

Peak Tourist Season

December to February.

High temperatures, busy attractions.

Quieter Months

June to August.

Hot weather, fewer tourists.

Avoid

May and September can be extremely hot.

Festivals and Events
