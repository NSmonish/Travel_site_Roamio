Things To Do In Doha

Must-Visit Places

The Corniche

Take a leisurely stroll along the Corniche, Doha's picturesque waterfront promenade. Enjoy breathtaking views of the city skyline, relax in beautiful parks, and explore attractions such as the Museum of Islamic Art and the Dhow Harbour.

The Pearl-Qatar

Visit The Pearl-Qatar, an artificial island featuring luxury residences, upscale shopping malls, and gourmet restaurants. Explore the Venetian-style canals, relax on pristine beaches, and admire the stunning architecture and waterfront views.

Souq Waqif

Explore Souq Waqif, Doha's vibrant traditional market. Wander through narrow alleyways lined with shops selling spices, textiles, and handicrafts. Experience the lively atmosphere, enjoy street performances, and savor delicious local cuisine at restaurants and caf√©s.

Museum of Islamic Art

Discover the treasures of Islamic art and culture at the Museum of Islamic Art. Admire a stunning collection of artifacts, including ceramics, textiles, and manuscripts, spanning over 1,400 years of Islamic history. Explore the museum's beautiful architecture and enjoy panoramic views of Doha's skyline.

Qatar National Museum

Visit the Qatar National Museum, a cultural landmark celebrating Qatar's heritage and history. Explore interactive exhibits, learn about the country's natural environment, and discover its rich cultural traditions. Don't miss the iconic desert rose-inspired architecture of the museum building.

Aspire Park

Experience the lush greenery of Aspire Park, one of the largest urban parks in Doha. Enjoy a relaxing walk or bike ride along the landscaped paths, have a picnic with family and friends, and admire the iconic Torch Doha hotel towering over the park.

Adventures

Embark on a thrilling desert safari adventure in the inland sea of Qatar. Enjoy exhilarating activities such as dune bashing, sandboarding, and camel riding, and experience the beauty of Qatar's desert landscape at sunset.

Explore the serene mangroves of Qatar on a kayaking adventure. Paddle through narrow channels, observe diverse wildlife, and enjoy the tranquility of these unique coastal ecosystems.

Discover the underwater world of Qatar with a scuba diving excursion. Dive into crystal-clear waters teeming with marine life, explore vibrant coral reefs, and encounter fascinating sea creatures in their natural habitat.
