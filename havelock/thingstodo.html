Things To Do In Havelock Island

Most Loved Places

Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach, located on Havelock Island in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, is a picturesque haven renowned for its pristine sands and vibrant marine life. Perfect for snorkeling, adventure activities, and nature walks, this accessible gem promises unforgettable experiences amidst stunning natural beauty.

Cellular Jail

The Cellular Jail in Port Blair, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, epitomizes India's independence struggle. Constructed by the British in the late 19th century, it housed political prisoners enduring unimaginable hardships. Now a national monument and museum, it honors the sacrifices of freedom fighters, serving as a solemn reminder of their unwavering spirit for liberty and justice.

Mount Harriet National Park

Mount Harriet National Park, located near Havelock Island in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, India, is a paradise for nature lovers. Offering stunning vistas, diverse flora, and fauna, it's perfect for scenic treks and wildlife spotting. Visitors can enjoy picnics, nature walks, and photography amidst its serene ambiance and panoramic views, making it an unforgettable destination in the Andaman Islands.

Barren Island

Barren Island, located near Havelock,is home to India's only active volcano. This remote island boasts rugged terrain and volcanic activity, offering visitors a rare glimpse into the forces of geological evolution. Boat tours allow for safe observation of its otherworldly beauty, making it a must-see destination for adventurers and nature enthusiasts.

Radhanagar Beach

Radhanagar Beach on Havelock Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, is hailed as one of Asia's finest. With its white sands, clear waters, and breathtaking sunsets, it's a paradise for beachgoers and photographers. Whether you're sunbathing, swimming, or strolling, Radhanagar Beach promises an unforgettable tropical retreat.

Neil's Cove

Neil's Cove, nestled near Havelock Island in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, is a serene escape known for its tranquil beaches and crystal-clear waters. This hidden gem offers soft sandy shores, perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation amidst breathtaking natural beauty. With its peaceful ambiance and untouched landscapes, Neil's Cove is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking solace.

Ross Island

Ross Island, renamed Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Island, lies near Havelock Island. Once a British administrative hub, it now stands as a captivating ruins site, blending history with natural beauty. Visitors can explore remnants of British architecture amidst lush greenery and serene beaches, making it a must-visit destination for history buffs and nature enthusiasts in the Havelock area.

Kalapathar Beach

Kalapathar Beach in Havelock Island is a serene paradise known for its stunning black rocks, soft white sands, and crystal-clear waters. Escape the crowds and unwind in this tranquil haven, where you can take leisurely walks along the shore, bask in the sun, and enjoy panoramic views of the Andaman Sea. It's the ideal spot for those seeking a peaceful retreat amidst nature's beauty.

Vijay Nagar Beach

Vijay Nagar Beach, also known as Beach No. 5, is a pristine stretch of coastline nestled in Havelock Island. Offering powdery white sands, turquoise waters, and lush greenery, it's a paradise for beach lovers. Relax under the shade of palm trees, swim in the calm waters, or simply soak in the breathtaking views. With its tranquil ambiance and natural beauty, Vijay Nagar Beach is the perfect spot to unwind and rejuvenate.

Adventures

Snorkeling in Havelock Island unveils a mesmerizing underwater world. With pristine waters and vibrant coral reefs, it's a haven for marine enthusiasts. Encounter colorful fish, graceful turtles, and stunning coral formations for an unforgettable aquatic adventure.

Explore Havelock Island's coastal charm through kayaking. Glide along serene waters, discovering hidden coves and vibrant marine life for a memorable adventure in nature.

Enjoy the adrenaline rush of banana boat rides in Havelock. Hold on tight as you bounce over the waves, creating unforgettable memories with friends and family.

Experience the thrill of scuba diving in Havelock. Explore vibrant reefs and encounter exotic marine life for an unforgettable underwater adventure.
