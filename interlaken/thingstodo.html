Things To Do In Interlaken

Must-Visit Places

Schynige Platte

Take a scenic journey aboard the Schynige Platte Railway to the summit of Schynige Platte. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau mountains, explore alpine gardens, and embark on hiking trails amidst stunning landscapes.

Harder Kulm

Ride the funicular to Harder Kulm, Interlaken's local mountain summit. Marvel at panoramic views of Interlaken, Lake Thun, and Lake Brienz from the viewing platform. Enjoy hiking trails, mountain biking, or dining at the restaurant with spectacular vistas.

Jungfraujoch

Embark on an unforgettable journey to Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe." Travel by cogwheel train to the highest railway station in Europe, situated amidst the Jungfrau-Aletsch UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore ice caves, walk on the Sphinx Observation Terrace, and admire the stunning Alpine scenery.

Giessbach Falls

Discover the majestic Giessbach Falls, a series of waterfalls cascading down a forested mountainside into Lake Brienz. Take a boat ride across the lake or hike through lush forests to reach the falls. Enjoy stunning views and experience the natural beauty of the Swiss Alps.

Lauterbrunnen Valley

Explore the picturesque Lauterbrunnen Valley, famous for its stunning waterfalls and dramatic cliffs. Take a leisurely stroll along the valley floor, visit iconic waterfalls like Staubbach Falls and Trümmelbach Falls, or embark on hiking trails leading to alpine meadows and panoramic viewpoints.

Saxeten

Discover the charming village of Saxeten, nestled in the mountains above Interlaken. Enjoy panoramic views of the Jungfrau region, go hiking or mountain biking on scenic trails, or simply relax and soak in the peaceful atmosphere of this alpine retreat.

Adventures

Experience the thrill of paragliding over Interlaken's stunning landscapes. Take off from scenic launch sites and soar through the sky with experienced pilots, enjoying breathtaking views of the Swiss Alps and shimmering lakes below.

Feel the adrenaline rush with a tandem skydiving experience over Interlaken. Jump from a plane at 15,000 feet and freefall at speeds of up to 200 kilometers per hour, enjoying breathtaking views of the Swiss Alps and scenic lakes before landing safely with your expert instructor.

Explore the pristine waters of Lake Thun and Lake Brienz with a canoeing or kayaking adventure. Paddle along tranquil shores, admire the surrounding mountain scenery, and discover hidden bays and beaches along the lakeshores.

Challenge yourself with rock climbing and via ferrata adventures in the Swiss Alps. Climb vertical cliffs, traverse thrilling via ferrata routes, and enjoy stunning views of Interlaken and the surrounding mountains from high above.
