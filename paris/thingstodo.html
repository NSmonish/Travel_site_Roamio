Things To Do In Paris

Must-Visit Places

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, a symbol of Paris, is an iconic landmark known for its breathtaking views and elegant design. Whether you're admiring it from afar or ascending to its observation decks, the Eiffel Tower offers a memorable experience.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, known for its stunning facade, intricate sculptures, and beautiful stained glass windows. Take a guided tour to learn about its history and significance in French culture.

Champs-Élysées

The Champs-Élysées is one of the most famous avenues in the world, lined with theaters, cafes, and luxury shops. Take a leisurely stroll, enjoy a coffee at a sidewalk cafe, or indulge in some retail therapy along this iconic boulevard.

Montmartre

Montmartre is a historic neighborhood known for its charming streets, artistic heritage, and stunning views of Paris. Visit the iconic Sacré-Cœur Basilica, explore the bustling Place du Tertre, and discover the vibrant arts scene of this picturesque district.

Seine River Cruise

Experience the beauty of Paris from the water with a Seine River cruise. Sail past iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum, while enjoying breathtaking views of the city's architecture and skyline.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most visited art museums, housing thousands of works, including the famous Mona Lisa. Explore its vast collection of art, artifacts, and sculptures, spanning thousands of years of history.

Disneyland Paris

Experience the magic of Disneyland Paris, where fairy tales come to life. Explore two enchanting theme parks, Disneyland Park and Walt Disney Studios Park, filled with thrilling rides, colorful parades, and beloved Disney characters.

Tour Montparnasse

For panoramic views of Paris, head to Tour Montparnasse. From its observation deck on the 56th floor, you can enjoy unparalleled views of the city, including the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Luxembourg Gardens

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city at Luxembourg Gardens, a peaceful oasis in the heart of Paris. Stroll through its lush green lawns, admire the beautiful flower beds and fountains, or relax with a book by the picturesque pond.

Adventures

Soar above Paris in a hot air balloon for a unique aerial perspective of the city's landmarks. Enjoy breathtaking views of iconic sights like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Seine River as you glide through the sky.

Embark on a bike tour of Paris to see the city's famous landmarks and hidden gems. Cycle along the Seine River, through picturesque neighborhoods, and past iconic sites like the Louvre and Place de la Concorde.

Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site renowned for its opulent architecture and beautiful gardens. Explore the palace's grand halls, lavish apartments, and stunning grounds, including the famous Hall of Mirrors.

Discover Paris on a Segway tour for a fun and exhilarating sightseeing experience. Glide past famous attractions, historic neighborhoods, and scenic parks while enjoying informative commentary from your guide.
