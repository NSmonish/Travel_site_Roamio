Restaurants in Paris!

Le Cinq

(1,921)
French,European

Indulge in exquisite French cuisine at Le Cinq, a Michelin-starred restaurant located in the luxurious Four Seasons Hotel George V. Experience fine dining at its best.

Pizzeria Arrivederci

(1,224)
Italian,Pizza,Mediterranean

Pizzeria Arrivederci offers authentic Italian cuisine with a focus on delicious pizzas. Located in the heart of Paris, near the Louvre Museum, it's a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike. The restaurant's cozy atmosphere and friendly service create a memorable dining experience.

Les Ombres

(1,354)
French

Located near the Eiffel Tower, Les Ombres offers panoramic views of Paris along with delicious French cuisine. Enjoy a memorable dining experience with a breathtaking backdrop.

Monsieur Bleu

(953)
French,European

Located in the Palais de Tokyo museum, Monsieur Bleu offers modern French cuisine in a chic and stylish setting. Enjoy gourmet dishes with a view of the Eiffel Tower.

La Passagère

(872)
French

Experience French gastronomy at its finest at La Passagère. Situated in the Hôtel Bel-Ami, this restaurant offers creative and delicious dishes in a sophisticated ambiance.

Chez Ajia

(975)
French, Asian Fusion, Taiwanese

Chez Ajia is a charming restaurant offering a unique fusion of French and Asian cuisine. Located in the vibrant Marais district, it's known for its exquisite seafood dishes and creative flavor combinations. With its cozy ambiance and attentive service, Chez Ajia promises an unforgettable dining experience in the heart of Paris.
