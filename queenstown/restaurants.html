Restaurants in Queenstown!

Rata

(1,576)
New Zealand, European,Contemporary, Fusion

Rata offers a unique dining experience with its innovative cuisine. Located in the heart of Queenstown, this restaurant showcases the best of local produce and flavors, creatively prepared by renowned chef Josh Emett.

Botswana Butchery

(1,224)
Steakhouse, European, Seafood

Botswana Butchery is renowned for its premium cuts of meat and stunning waterfront location. Enjoy fine dining in a rustic yet elegant atmosphere, with dishes inspired by New Zealand's rich culinary heritage.

Remarkables Market Kitchen

(972)
Cafe,New Zealand,Contemporary

Experience casual dining with stunning mountain views at Remarkables Market Kitchen. Using locally sourced ingredients, this cafe offers a range of delicious dishes, from hearty breakfasts to gourmet burgers and salads.

Eichardt's Bar

(857)
European, Bar, New Zealand

Eichardt's Bar offers a sophisticated dining experience in a historic setting. Indulge in modern cuisine, paired with fine wines and craft cocktails, while enjoying views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables mountain range.

Amisfield Bistro

(742)
European, New Zealand

Located in a picturesque vineyard setting, Amisfield Bistro offers a seasonal menu featuring fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Enjoy innovative dishes paired with award-winning wines from Central Otago.

Saffron Restaurant

(685)
European, Indian,Contemporary

Saffron Restaurant offers a unique fusion of Indian and European cuisine, showcasing the best of both worlds. Located in the heart of Queenstown, it's a favorite among locals and visitors for its bold flavors and warm hospitality.
