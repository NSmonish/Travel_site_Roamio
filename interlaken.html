Welcome To Interlaken!

Interlaken, nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, is a charming town in the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland. Surrounded by stunning mountain peaks, including the Eiger, MÃ¶nch, and Jungfrau, Interlaken is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. Whether you're interested in hiking, skiing, paragliding, or simply enjoying the scenic beauty, Interlaken has something for everyone.

Experience the beauty and adventure of Interlaken with its wide range of activities and attractions. Explore the picturesque villages of Lauterbrunnen and Grindelwald, take a thrilling ride on the Jungfraujoch railway, or cruise on the crystal-clear waters of Lake Thun. With its breathtaking landscapes and vibrant culture, Interlaken promises an unforgettable experience.
