Things To Do In Cape Town

Must-Visit Places

Table Mountain

Ride the cable car or hike to the top of Table Mountain for spectacular views of Cape Town and its surroundings. Explore hiking trails, marvel at unique flora and fauna, and enjoy a meal at the mountaintop caf√© while taking in the scenery.

Boulders Beach

Visit Boulders Beach to see African penguins in their natural habitat. Walk along the boardwalks and observe these adorable birds as they swim, waddle, and bask in the sun. Enjoy a swim in the sheltered bay and relax on the sandy shores.

Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens

Explore Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens, renowned for its diverse plant species and stunning landscapes. Discover indigenous flora, walk along scenic paths, and relax in beautiful garden settings with views of Table Mountain.

Robben Island

Take a ferry to Robben Island, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a rich history. Tour the prison where Nelson Mandela was incarcerated, learn about South Africa's struggle for freedom, and explore the island's natural and cultural landmarks.

Victoria & Alfred Waterfront

Visit the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, a vibrant shopping and entertainment hub in Cape Town. Explore shops, restaurants, and attractions, take a boat trip to Robben Island, or simply enjoy the lively atmosphere and beautiful harbor views.

Cape Point Nature Reserve

Explore Cape Point Nature Reserve and the Cape of Good Hope, located at the southwestern tip of the Cape Peninsula. Discover scenic viewpoints, coastal trails, and diverse wildlife in this rugged and picturesque natural reserve.

Adventures

Experience the thrill of shark cage diving off the coast of Cape Town. Get up close and personal with great white sharks in their natural habitat, protected by a secure cage. Witness these magnificent creatures in action and create unforgettable memories.

Hike up Lion's Head for panoramic views of Cape Town, Table Mountain, and the Atlantic Seaboard. Enjoy a moderately challenging hike with stunning scenery along the way. Reach the summit for breathtaking 360-degree views of the city and coastline.

Embark on a scenic helicopter flight over Cape Town and the Cape Peninsula. Soar above iconic landmarks like Table Mountain, Cape Point, and the Twelve Apostles, enjoying unparalleled views of the city and its breathtaking natural beauty.
