Restaurants in Cape Town

Haute Cabrière Restaurant

(1,123)
French, European, Wine Bar

Haute Cabrière Restaurant offers French-inspired cuisine and an extensive selection of wines in a beautiful setting overlooking the Franschhoek Valley. Enjoy gourmet dishes paired with fine wines produced by the estate.

The Bungalow

(724)
Seafood, Contemporary, Cocktail Bar

The Bungalow offers a stylish dining experience right on the beachfront with panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean. Enjoy fresh seafood dishes, innovative cocktails, and a vibrant atmosphere at this popular Cape Town restaurant.

Catharina's Restaurant

(603)
European, Contemporary, Fine Dining

Catharina's Restaurant offers an elegant dining experience on the historic Steenberg Wine Estate. Indulge in European-inspired cuisine made with seasonal ingredients sourced from local suppliers, all served in a beautifully restored manor house.

The Test Kitchen

(1,548)
Fine Dining, Contemporary, African

The Test Kitchen is a renowned fine dining restaurant offering innovative and creative dishes inspired by African flavors. Located in the Old Biscuit Mill, this restaurant provides an intimate and exclusive culinary experience.

Kloof Street House

(1,327)
European, Contemporary, Bar

Kloof Street House offers a charming and eclectic dining experience in a Victorian-era house. The menu features European-inspired dishes with a contemporary twist, and the bar serves a wide selection of cocktails and wines.

Belthazar

(1,821)
Steakhouse, Seafood, Wine Bar

Belthazar is a premier steakhouse and wine bar located in the vibrant V&A Waterfront. Offering an extensive wine selection and the finest cuts of meat and fresh seafood, it's a haven for food and wine lovers.
