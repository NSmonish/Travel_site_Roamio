Welcome To Krabi!

Krabi, located on the west coast of southern Thailand, is renowned for its stunning beaches, limestone cliffs, and vibrant marine life. With its laid-back atmosphere and natural beauty, Krabi is a paradise for beach lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Explore the iconic Railay Beach, known for its crystal-clear waters and towering limestone formations. Discover hidden lagoons, go rock climbing, or simply relax on the pristine beaches.

Best Time To Visit

November to March.

Dry season, pleasant weather.

Peak Tourist Season

December to February.

High temperatures, crowded beaches.

Quieter Months

May to October.

Rainy season, fewer tourists.

Avoid

July to October can have heavy rainfall.
