Things To Do In Krabi

Must-Visit Places

Ao Nang Beach

Relax at Ao Nang Beach, one of Krabi's most popular beaches. Enjoy the soft sand, crystal-clear waters, and stunning limestone cliffs. You can also explore nearby islands on a boat tour or indulge in water sports like snorkeling and kayaking.

Tup Island

Visit Tup Island, known for its unique sandbar that emerges during low tide, connecting it with Chicken Island. Enjoy walking across the sandbar, swimming in the clear waters, and exploring the island's coral reefs, perfect for snorkeling.

Railay Beach

Explore the stunning Railay Beach, accessible only by boat due to its dramatic limestone cliffs. Relax on the white sand, go rock climbing on the cliffs, and visit Phra Nang Cave Beach, known for its fascinating cave and princess shrine.

Emerald Pool

Take a refreshing dip in the Emerald Pool, a natural spring surrounded by lush rainforest in Thung Teao Forest Natural Park. Enjoy the clear emerald-green waters and the tranquil atmosphere of this unique swimming spot.

Krabi Town Night Market

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the Krabi Town Night Market. Explore stalls selling local handicrafts, clothing, and souvenirs, and sample a variety of delicious street food, including fresh seafood, Thai curries, and tropical fruits.

Krabi Hot Springs

Relax in the natural hot springs of Krabi, surrounded by lush jungle. Soak in the warm mineral-rich waters, known for their therapeutic properties, and enjoy the serene ambiance of the tropical forest.

Adventures

Go rock climbing in Railay Beach, one of the world's premier rock climbing destinations. Climb limestone cliffs with breathtaking views of the Andaman Sea and enjoy thrilling routes suitable for climbers of all levels.

Embark on a jungle trekking adventure in Khao Sok National Park, located just a few hours from Krabi. Explore ancient rainforests, discover hidden waterfalls, and encounter diverse wildlife, including monkeys, elephants, and exotic birds.

Experience the beauty of Krabi's coastline on a sea kayaking tour. Paddle through mangrove forests, explore hidden lagoons and caves, and discover secluded beaches accessible only by kayak.
