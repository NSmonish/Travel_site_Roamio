Things To Do In Istanbul

Must-Visit Places

Bosphorus Cruise

Take a scenic cruise along the Bosphorus Strait, passing iconic landmarks like the Dolmabahce Palace, Ortakoy Mosque, and Maiden's Tower. Enjoy breathtaking views of Istanbul's skyline and witness the unique blend of European and Asian cultures.

Hagia Sophia

Explore the magnificent Hagia Sophia, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and former cathedral and mosque. Admire its stunning architecture, intricate mosaics, and rich history spanning over 1,500 years.

Grand Bazaar

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the Grand Bazaar, one of the world's oldest and largest covered markets. Browse through thousands of shops selling carpets, spices, jewelry, and other unique treasures.

Topkapi Palace

Step back in time at Topkapi Palace, the former residence of Ottoman sultans for over 400 years. Explore its opulent courtyards, stunning architecture, and impressive collections of artifacts, including the famous Topkapi Dagger and Spoonmaker's Diamond.

Blue Mosque

Visit the iconic Blue Mosque, known for its magnificent blue tiles and six towering minarets. Admire its stunning architecture and intricate interior decorations, including its vast prayer hall and beautiful courtyard.

Spice Bazaar

Immerse yourself in the sights, sounds, and smells of the Spice Bazaar, also known as the Egyptian Bazaar. Discover a wide variety of spices, teas, dried fruits, and Turkish delights while exploring this bustling market.

Adventures

Experience the thrill of a hot air balloon ride over Cappadocia's stunning landscapes. Soar high above fairy chimneys, ancient rock formations, and picturesque valleys for an unforgettable adventure.

Embark on a sunset dinner cruise along the Bosphorus Strait. Enjoy delicious Turkish cuisine, live entertainment, and panoramic views of Istanbul's skyline as the sun sets over the water.

Witness the mesmerizing Whirling Dervishes ceremony, a spiritual dance performance that symbolizes the mystical journey of the soul. Experience the beauty and grace of this ancient Sufi tradition.

Discover the underwater world of the Mediterranean Sea with a scuba diving adventure in Kas. Dive among colorful coral reefs, ancient shipwrecks, and a variety of marine life in this popular diving destination.
