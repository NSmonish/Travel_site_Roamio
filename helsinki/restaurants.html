Restaurants in Helsinki!

Suomenlinnan Panimo & Ravintola

(1,123)
Finnish, European, Brewery,Scandinavian

Suomenlinnan Panimo & Ravintola offers a unique dining experience on the historic Suomenlinna Island. Enjoy delicious Finnish and European cuisine along with a variety of craft beers brewed on-site, all while taking in stunning views of the Helsinki harbor.

Spis

(982)
Nordic, Scandinavian,European

Spis offers a unique culinary experience with its Nordic and Scandinavian-inspired dishes. Situated in a cozy setting, this restaurant focuses on using locally sourced, organic ingredients to create innovative and delicious meals that reflect the flavors of the region.

Ravintola Skiffer

(724)
Pizza, Italian, European

Ravintola Skiffer is famous for its authentic Italian-style pizzas made with high-quality ingredients and cooked in a wood-fired oven. Located near the waterfront, this cozy pizzeria offers a relaxed atmosphere and stunning views of the sea.

Zetor

(603)
Finnish, European, Barbecue

Zetor offers a taste of Finnish culture with its hearty Finnish cuisine and rustic decor. This restaurant is known for its grilled specialties, such as reindeer and salmon, as well as its selection of Finnish beers and spirits.

Sunshine Bar & Grill

(1,548)
American, Bar, Grill

Sunshine Bar & Grill offers American-style comfort food in a laid-back atmosphere. Located by the waterfront, this restaurant serves up classic dishes like burgers, ribs, and steaks, along with a wide selection of cocktails and craft beers.

Latva Bar & Bistro

(1,472)
European, Finnish, Scandinavian

Latva Bar & Bistro offers a cozy atmosphere and a menu inspired by Finnish and Scandinavian flavors. Located in the city center, this restaurant is known for its seasonal dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
