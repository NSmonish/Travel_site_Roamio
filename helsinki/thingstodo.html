Things To Do In Helsinki

Must-Visit Places

Helsinki Cathedral

Marvel at the stunning architecture of Helsinki Cathedral, one of Finland's most iconic landmarks. Located in the heart of the city, this neoclassical cathedral boasts striking white domes and a grand facade.

Suomenlinna Sea Fortress

Explore the historic Suomenlinna Sea Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located on a group of islands just off the coast of Helsinki. Discover centuries of history as you wander through fortress walls, tunnels, and museums.

Temppeliaukio Church

Visit the unique Temppeliaukio Church, also known as the Rock Church, carved directly into solid rock. Admire the modern architectural design and serene atmosphere of this popular Helsinki attraction.

Senate Square

Take a stroll through Senate Square, the historic center of Helsinki. Admire the neoclassical buildings surrounding the square, including the Government Palace, Helsinki University, and the National Library of Finland.

Esplanadi Park

Relax in the picturesque Esplanadi Park, a green oasis in the heart of Helsinki. Enjoy a leisurely walk among lush gardens, charming cafes, and lively street performers.

Nuuksio National Park

Immerse yourself in the pristine nature of Nuuksio National Park, located just outside of Helsinki. Explore peaceful forests, crystal-clear lakes, and rugged landscapes on hiking trails suitable for all skill levels.

Adventures

Embark on a scenic cruise through the beautiful archipelago of Helsinki. Relax on deck as you glide past picturesque islands, historic landmarks, and scenic coastline.

Experience the thrill of ice fishing on the frozen lakes of Helsinki. Learn traditional Finnish fishing techniques and try your luck catching perch, pike, and trout beneath the ice.

Discover the magic of Finnish sauna culture with a traditional sauna experience in Helsinki. Relax in a wood-fired sauna, enjoy refreshing dips in the sea or a cooling lake, and unwind like a true Finn.

Escape the city and enjoy a day of skiing or snowboarding in the surrounding mountains of Helsinki. With several ski resorts nearby, you can experience thrilling runs and stunning alpine scenery.

Experience the exhilaration of dog sledding through the snowy wilderness of Helsinki. Bundle up in warm blankets as a team of huskies pulls you through magical forests and open plains.
