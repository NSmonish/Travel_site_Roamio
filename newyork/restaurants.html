Restaurants in New York

Gramercy Tavern

(2,224)
American, Farm-to-Table

Gramercy Tavern is known for its seasonal American cuisine sourced from local farms. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, it's a great place to enjoy dishes made with fresh, quality ingredients.

Jean-Georges

(1,354)
French, Contemporary

Jean-Georges is a world-renowned French restaurant offering contemporary cuisine with global influences. Located in a chic setting, it's perfect for a fine dining experience in New York.

Katz's Delicatessen

(953)
Jewish, Deli, Kosher

Katz's Delicatessen is a New York institution known for its classic Jewish deli fare, including pastrami and corned beef sandwiches. It's a must-visit for anyone craving authentic deli food.

Ippudo

(872)
Japanese, Ramen, Asian

Ippudo is a popular ramen restaurant offering delicious bowls of tonkotsu ramen. With its flavorful broth and perfectly cooked noodles, it's a favorite among ramen enthusiasts in New York.

La Pecora Bianca

(975)
Italian, Farm-to-Table

La Pecora Bianca offers modern Italian cuisine made with locally sourced ingredients. From homemade pastas to wood-fired pizzas, it's a great spot for a casual Italian meal in New York.

Levain Bakery

(834)
Bakery, Desserts

Levain Bakery is famous for its giant cookies with a gooey center and crispy edges. If you have a sweet tooth, this bakery is a must-visit for some of the best cookies in New York.
